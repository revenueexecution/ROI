How are these numbers calculated? ▾

Cost of underperformance — Revenue gap divided across your underperforming sellers, multiplied by implied commission rate derived from OTE and target.

Business Development Accelerator — 3 additional net new meetings per month per seller for months remaining, applied to close rate and average deal value.

Opportunity Development Accelerator — Applied to pipeline per seller. 30% more pipeline closed through sharper qualification, plus 20% deal value uplift through improved value discovery.

Deal Progression Accelerator — 3-week cycle reduction applied to pipeline per seller, plus 50% recovery of late-stage losses divided across sellers.

Team totals — Per-seller figures multiplied across full team size. Programme cost comparison uses published pricing tiers.
