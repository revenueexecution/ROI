How are these numbers calculated? ▾

Business Development Accelerator — Based on 3 additional net new meetings per month for the months remaining in your year, applied to your close rate and average deal value.

Opportunity Development Accelerator — Applied to your existing pipeline value. Assumes 30% more of your pipeline closed through sharper qualification, plus 20% uplift in deal value through improved value discovery and price holding.

Deal Progression Accelerator — Applied to your existing pipeline. Assumes 3-week reduction in sales cycle plus recovery of 50% of deals you lost late stage last quarter.

Management Review Accelerator — The value here is not a revenue number. It is the protection of your position and the time to recover.
